How it works

Four steps from inquiry to signed contract.

Build the pricing

Pull products from your catalog, add scenarios, and apply per-line discounts. Totals, tax, and margins calculate instantly.

Write the proposal

Let AI draft the whole proposal from your pricing — in your brand voice — drop in live pricing tables, and brand it with your logo and font.

Send for signature

Preview the PDF, then send for e-signature. Your client signs online; you counter-sign — no printing, no scanning.

Track & win

Watch status flip from sent → viewed → signed in real time, with a dashboard of pipeline value and win rate.
