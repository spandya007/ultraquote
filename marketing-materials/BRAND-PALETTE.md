# SmartProps Brand Palette — Green / Lime

Rebranded 2026-07-21. **Lime green** is the primary brand/action color (matching the
app's `--primary`); a brighter **leaf green** is the accent for section eyebrows,
highlights, icon tiles, and decorative detail. Navy is the dark surface (covers, footers).
Sourced from the SmartProps logo greens (`#8FC24E` lime · `#2C5A0E` · `#1B3D08`).

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Brand Lime | `#4D7C0F` | Primary buttons, headline highlight, links, featured plan |
| Primary (dark) | Brand Lime Dark | `#3D640D` | Hover / pressed states |
| Accent | Leaf Green | `#65A30D` | Eyebrows, accent bars, icon tints, secondary buttons, step numbers |
| Accent (text) | Green Dark | `#3F6212` | Green text on light green fills |
| Accent (fill) | Green Mist | `#F0F7E0` | Icon tile backgrounds, light accent sections |
| Accent (badge) | Green 100 | `#DCF0C2` | "Beta" badge fill (with Green Dark text) |
| Accent (glow) | Logo Lime | `#8FC24E` | Logo-mark gradient end, bright highlights |
| Ink | Navy | `#0B1F3A` | Dark backgrounds, primary headings |
| Neutral | Slate | `#64748B` | Body / muted text |
| Neutral | Line | `#E2E8F0` | Borders / dividers |
| Neutral | Cloud | `#F8FAFC` | Light surface fills |
| Semantic | Success | `#4D7C0F` | "You save / signed / won" states |
| Surface | White | `#FFFFFF` | Base background |

Brand gradient (covers/CTA): `linear-gradient(135deg, #4D7C0F → #65A30D)`.
Note: the in-app brand color (`--primary` in `app/globals.css`) is lime `#4d7c0f` (light) /
`#84cc16` (dark) — this marketing palette matches it. The in-app "Forest" accent swatch
stays `#16a34a` (green-600), deliberately distinct from the Default.
